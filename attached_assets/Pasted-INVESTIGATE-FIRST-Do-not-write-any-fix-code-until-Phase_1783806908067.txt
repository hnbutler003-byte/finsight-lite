CONTEXT: The Investment Simulator lets students buy/sell stocks and bonds with a virtual starting balance of B$10,000. Two suspected bugs need root-cause diagnosis before fixing:

BUG 1 — Virtual Cash balance does not decrement after a purchase.
Example: starting balance B$10,000. Student buys 10 shares of BTC-BS at B$5.25 avg (-B$52.50) and 5 shares of CBL-BS at B$7.90 avg (-B$39.50). Recent Trades ledger correctly shows both debits. Virtual Cash card still displays B$10,000.00 instead of the expected ~B$9,908.

BUG 2 — Price mismatch between the "Real Market Prices" BISX ticker strip and the actual tradable Buy/Sell cards for the same stock.
Example: CBL (Commonwealth Bank) shows B$17.00 on the ticker strip but B$8.09 on the Buy card. FCL shows B$11.50 on the strip but B$3.73 on the Buy card. These should be the same number, sourced from the same feed.
