This is a comprehensive, full-platform functional audit. The goal is to click through and test every page, game, and interactive feature in the app, exactly as a real student, teacher, or admin would, and fix anything found broken, sluggish, or behaving incorrectly. Do not skip anything because it seems unlikely to have issues. Work through this in stages, report findings before fixing within each stage.

Run this only after the prior priority fixes (git sync, upload hardening, org approval gate) are confirmed complete, since this audit assumes those are stable.

---

STAGE 1 — Student-facing pages and features

For each of the following, actually load the page/feature as a test student and verify real functionality, not just that it renders:

1. / (avatar login) — confirm avatar selection works, login succeeds, and a returning student lands correctly.
2. /demo — confirm the demo picker (already fixed earlier) still works correctly post recent changes.
3. /budgets — create a budget, add an expense, confirm the chart updates correctly, confirm input validation (no negative numbers, no absurd values) works as built earlier.
4. /savings — create a savings goal, log progress, confirm the progress bar updates correctly and the completion celebration fires at 100%.
5. /invest — test both BISX and JSE ticker views, confirm switching territory/currency shows the correct stock set, confirm any buy/sell simulation logic works and updates the portfolio correctly, confirm dark mode ticker visibility (recently fixed) holds.
6. /games — play through each of the seven money games at least briefly. Confirm each game starts, can be played to completion or a meaningful stopping point, scores/feedback display correctly, and no game freezes or crashes, especially under a simulated slow connection if testable.
7. /guide — ask the Money Guide chatbot a real, on-topic question and confirm it responds correctly and the "Thinking it through..." loading state fires correctly.
8. /lessons — open at least one lesson per the three core modules, confirm content displays, confirm the Sand Dollar module (once territory-gated, per separate task) only appears for Bahamas accounts, confirm quiz submission and scoring works, confirm the AI Tutor auto-routing on low scores (built earlier) fires correctly.
9. /moneylab, /moneylab/upload, /moneylab/play, /moneylab/tutor, /moneylab/leaderboard — confirm exam paper upload works within its file validation rules, confirm AI-generated quiz questions are coherent and usable, confirm the AI tutor/explanation mode responds correctly, confirm the leaderboard displays and updates correctly.
10. /settings — confirm account settings display and save correctly, confirm the account deletion flow (built earlier) still works correctly with its confirmation step.
