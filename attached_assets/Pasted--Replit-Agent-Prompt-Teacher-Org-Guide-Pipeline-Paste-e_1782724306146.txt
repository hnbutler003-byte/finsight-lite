```markdown
# Finsight Lite — Organization Admin Guide

## Getting Started

**Your portal:** https://finsightlite.com/org/login
This is separate from the student app, teacher portal, and founder admin
dashboard. Share this link only with other admins in your organization.

### Creating Your Admin Account
First time? Go to https://finsightlite.com/org/register instead of logging in.

You'll need:
- First and last name
- Email address (becomes your login)
- Password (minimum 6 characters)
- Your Organization Join Code — the 6-character code identifying your
  organization (e.g. 7TBMKQ). Tied to a specific environment (school,
  cohort, or class group). Don't have it? Contact hailey@finsight-ltd.com.

Once registered, you're signed in automatically. Sessions last 7 days.

![Org registration screen](./screenshots/org-register-light.png)

## The Dashboard

Land here after login: https://finsightlite.com/org/dashboard

**Stats row (4 cards):**
| Card | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Env Students | Students enrolled in your specific environment |
| Org Students | Total students across all environments in your org |
| Environments | How many separate environments your org has |
| Published Lessons | Lessons published and visible to students |

**Student Join Code card** — your environment's join code in large text.
This is what students enter to join. Use the copy button to grab it for
emails, slides, or messages.

**Environment Info card** — display name, URL slug, and lesson count for
your current environment.

**All Environments panel** (only shows if your org has more than one
environment) — table of every environment: name, join code, student count.
Your current one is highlighted.

**Organization Details** — your org's name, type, and country.

![Org dashboard](./screenshots/org-dashboard-light.png)

## Managing Students

Sidebar → Students. Shows every student across your *entire* organization,
not just your environment. Each row shows: avatar + name, username, level
badge, streak 🔥, XP, joined date, and an environment tag (indigo badge).

**Removing a student:** trash icon on the row → confirm. This unenrolls
them from the organization entirely.

Students enroll themselves with your join code — you never add them
manually.

![Students page](./screenshots/org-students-light.png)

## Creating and Managing Lessons

Sidebar → Lessons. Lists all lesson plans, published and draft clearly
labeled.

**New lesson — Step 1, Lesson Details:**
Title (required), Instructor, Grade Level, Subject (defaults to "Financial
Literacy"), Duration, Topic, Learning Objectives (one per line), Content
Section Heading + Body.

**New lesson — Step 2, Quiz Questions (optional):**
Question text, Options A–D, click the correct letter to mark it (highlights
blue). "Add Another Question" for more. Correct answer defaults to A —
always click the right one. "Skip for Now" saves without a quiz.

**Publishing:** click Publish on a lesson card → label changes to
Published, border turns blue, students in your environment can now see it.
Click Unpublish to take it back to Draft.

**Viewing details:** click the chevron (▾) on a lesson card to expand
objectives and content sections.

![Lesson creation flow](./screenshots/org-lesson-create-light.png)

## Sidebar Navigation

| Icon | Page | For |
|---|---|---|
| Grid | Dashboard | Overview stats, join code, environment info |
| Users | Students | View and manage enrolled students |
| Book | Lessons | Create, edit, publish lesson plans |

Your name, email, and environment name show at the bottom. Click Sign Out
to log out safely.

## Tips

- Share your join code early — students need it the moment they open
  Finsight Lite.
- Draft first, publish when ready.
- Objectives and quiz questions feed directly into student completion
  scoring and XP — don't skip them.
- Student XP and levels update in real time on the Students page.

## FAQ / Troubleshooting

**A student says their join code isn't working.**
Confirm they're using the *environment* join code shown on your Dashboard,
not an org name or another environment's code.

**I don't see the "All Environments" panel.**
That panel only appears if your organization has more than one environment
set up. Single-environment orgs won't see it — that's expected.

**I published a lesson but a student says they can't see it.**
Confirm the student is enrolled in the *same environment* the lesson was
published to — lessons are environment-scoped, not org-wide.

**Still stuck?** Use the help chat icon in the bottom corner of any portal
page — it has the full contents of this guide and can usually answer
faster than searching here.
```
