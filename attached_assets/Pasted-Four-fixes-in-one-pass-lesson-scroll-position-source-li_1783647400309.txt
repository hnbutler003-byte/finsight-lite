PART 4: BISX REAL MARKET PRICES ARE INACCURATE
============================================================

This needs investigation before a fix, the exact nature of the inaccuracy isn't
specified yet. Diagnose first:

1. Confirm where the Investment Simulator's real BISX price data actually comes
   from, a live API, a scheduled scrape, or a manually updated data source.
2. Check when that data source was last successfully updated, compare the
   timestamp against today's actual date. If it's stale, that's likely the root
   cause, not a calculation bug.
3. Check whether the six tracked tickers (CBL, FCL, CAB, DHS, JSJ, CHB) are
   still correctly mapped to real BISX data, a ticker rename, delisting, or
   symbol mismatch on BISX's side could cause wrong prices to display under the
   wrong ticker.
4. Check for a hardcoded fallback/seed price still being used anywhere instead
   of live data.
5. Check currency and decimal formatting, a formatting bug (e.g. displaying
   cents as dollars, or a stale currency conversion) can look like "wrong
   prices" without the underlying data actually being wrong.

Report what you find for each of these before applying a fix, since the correct
fix depends entirely on which of these is the actual cause. Do not guess at a
fix without first confirming the root cause.
